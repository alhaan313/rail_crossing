Help & Tips
Quick guide to use RailGate safely and easily.

Understand the status

  • ✅ Gate Open — Road is open.
  • ⚠️ Closing Soon — Train is near. Slow down.
  • 🚧 Gate Closed — Please wait till it opens.

Mobile tips

  • Use A+ / A- (top-right) to change text size.
  • Use the big buttons at the bottom to switch pages.
  • On Crossings, tap Directions for quick navigation.

Safety

  • Never cross when the gate is closing or closed.
  • Watch for a second train on another track.
  • Emergency helpline (Railways): 139

Why times can differ

Times are estimates from public sources. Trains may be early/late. Always follow signals and staff.
